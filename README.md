# Cron Expression Builder

A visual tool for building cron expressions. Pick your schedule from dropdowns instead of trying to remember the syntax — the app generates the expression and shows you exactly when it'll run.

## What It Does

- **Dropdown selectors** for minute, hour, day of month, month, and weekday
- **Live cron expression** updates as you change options
- **Human-readable description** — "Every Monday at 9:00 AM"
- **Next 5 run times** so you can verify the schedule is right
- **Common presets** — every minute, hourly, daily, weekly, monthly

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Use the dropdowns to pick your schedule
3. Copy the cron expression
4. Paste it into your crontab, CI/CD pipeline, or scheduler

No build step. No dependencies. Just open and use.
